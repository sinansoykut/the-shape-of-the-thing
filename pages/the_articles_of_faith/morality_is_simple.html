the articles of faith

morality is simple


morality isn't a puzzle

when you act with care,
making relief, joy, or comfort in the hearts of others,
you create goodness

when you act with malice,
making pain, fear, or suffering,
you create badness

if it makes a person's inner world brighter, it's good
if it darkens their world, it's bad

acting morally means weighing all of our experiences together,
and choosing to be good, each time
