the articles of faith

the one true good


our subjective experience is the one true good
everything that happens in the world? value-neutral

we create value, good and bad, in the act experience

if a tree falls unwitnessed in the forest,
nothing salient has happened

if a tree falls on a person crushing them,
their suffering makes it bad

if the tree falls beside a person revealing a beautiful vista,
their awe makes it good

we are the measure of matter
