the articles of faith

our differences are cultural


we are plastic thinking machines,
our brains change and develop based on what we experience

we live in a world filled with pervasive gender norms,
these norms shape us as we grow, 
writing pathways into our brains
they incline and disincline us,
molding us until we fit well enough in the greater culture

the substantial gender differences we perceive are not innate,
they're culturally inculcated, 
picked up by us as we make our way through the world

we are not resigned to biological determinism,
we can change our culture and ourselves
