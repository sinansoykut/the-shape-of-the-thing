the articles of faith

our needs are the same


regardless of age, gender, race, or class,
all human people share the same set of needs

men do not need more competence or autonomy than women,
women do not need more emotional capacity
or connectedness than men

we are all labouring after the same needs,
safety, connection, autonomy, growth

while we are confined to our own direct experience,
the joys and sorrow of others
are analogous reflections of our own

everyone out here is reaching for the same thing,
we have unity of purpose, even as we divide ourselves

we are here together, let us act together
