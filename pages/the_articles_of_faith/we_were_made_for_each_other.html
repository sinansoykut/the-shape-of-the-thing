the articles of faith

we were made for each other


we are social creatures, with innate social desires,
we exist and make meaning in relation to each other

our pains and pleasures are personally and directly felt,
yet they echo in the hearts of those who witness them

being centered and receiving care feels good,
just as centering another and providing them care feels good

we were made to build our great house together,
not to compete in bickering isolation

our needs and abilities are bound together,
you have what I need, I have what you need

my friends, we were made for each other
