the articles of faith

we were made for each other


we are social creatures
with innate appetites
among our defining features
is to be seen in other's sights

our pleasures and pains
are personally known
and refusal to see others
will keep you alone

being centered and receiving care
feels good, and feels fair
giving kindness and holding grace
feels good, like an embrace

we were made to build
our great house together
our promise fulfilled
shelters us from the weather

our needs and abilities are intertwined
to give and receive, what we have in kind
my friends, for this, we were made
to join hands in our parade
