the articles of faith

equality of experience


each of us has an internal experience,
it feels special because we live inside it

but everyone else has an inner world,
just as vivid and profound as ours

we feel our own emotions deeply,
but only glimpse the internal states of others

that difference can fool us.
it makes our experience appear singular, 
as if ours is the only real thing

but no person's experience is paramount
what you feel is as deeply felt as what I feel

our lives are equal in the vibrancy of our inner worlds
