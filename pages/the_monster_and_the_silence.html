the shape of the thing

the monster and the silence


let me try again
to uncynically be seen
to speak to you as though
it could have ever been

when I was a child
I took a look around
and saw that everybody
suffered the same frown

when I was told to smile
while feeling deeply sad
I made a separation
of in and outer halves

and this thing without
that walks around with you
was faked until 'twas made
a simulacrum grew

all the while the one within
sublimated into ether
until there was nothing left
but a bare perceiver

we continue to persist
the monster and the silence
each a discrete half
bound in our defiance
