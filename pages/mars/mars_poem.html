mars


the bespoke god of man we've made
goes by the name of mars
and his consuming hunger 
demands offerings of ours

mars the god of man
of virility and war
mars who does demand
that an oath to him be swore

the patriarch
and the provider
our warlord king
who does preside here

mars who plays pretend
to a platonic form
but who is created
as we offer up our norms

only a god in so far
as he's got us believing
there's one way to appear
to be seen as achieving

his power is made
in the social domain
as we tithe to him
our care and our pain

men enter to his congregation
with selfish acts of mutilation
of those who kneel, and those who resist
we break on the wheel, or simply desist

this old man has had his time
and now his time is done
he's ours to grant or to decline
to make into someone
